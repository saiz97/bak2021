spa for a bachelor thesis

The purpose

This application was created in the course of a bachelor thesis from the study program "Communication and Knowledge Media", at the University of Applied Sciences Upper Austria, Campus Hagenberg.

The topic of the thesis is "End-to-End Testing of Angular Single-Page Applications". More precisely, the two testing frameworks Cypress and Protractor are to be compared with each other in the course of the work.

The application

This single-page application was implemented with Angular. All data about comics and characters displayed in this application is accessed by the Marvel Comics API. (MORE ABOUT MARVEL)

To acknowledge those creators of the other images used, here is a special thanks to Van dos Santos of Pexels and Erik Mclean of Unsplash.

Registered and logged in users have the possibility to favorite comics. Favorited comics are stored in a separate nosql database. Google Firebase is used for this purpose.

Stored data will not be used for statistics or analysis, nor will it be shared with third parties. This application serves solely as a practical example for the bachelor thesis of Daniel Saiz.
